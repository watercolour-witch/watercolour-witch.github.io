The Red Mandala of Joy

I painted this mandala after a long period of illness and depression. I felt stuck, so I decided to paint. Not because needed a solution for my problems but because I always enjoyed painting.

While I was creating this mandala- without any effort, without any agenda-my bad mood suddenly disappeared. This mandala has opened a doorway to joy, ease and happiness.

And I can reassure you if it has helped me it can help you too bring happiness into your daily life.

Use this mandala if:
  • You feel stuck or depressed
  • You want to let go of the negativity around you
  • You search an easy, eye-opening solution for your problems
  • You want to feel light and joyful
