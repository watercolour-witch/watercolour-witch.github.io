Deep inside you know there is something more.
You were not born to here just to live a boring, ordinary life. So, let's start with some good news:

You are here for enlightenment

In this lifetime, in your current body, on this planet. Darling, your time has come to ascend!


Oh, I hear you argue loud and clear...

Let me tell you a tiny, little secret: You are already awakened. You can do anything that you want. You are that you are.


And actually, you are pretty dang awesome!

But you might be unaware of your true, divine self.

But... but... but... - I still hear you - It's impossible for me to be enlightened, because...


Just shut up!

I’m fully aware of your problems and self-doubts!

I know that tons of sufferings and pains are dragging your shoulders down. I also know that you've spent tons of money and wasted bazillions of past lives on practicing various spiritual stuffs. You've tried out all the old methods and you've dug into the new age bullshizz as well. And it doesn’t matter that you've even tried to...

  • Become an organic wheatgrass juiceaholic
  • Collect more singing bowls than a Tibetan monk
  • Wear more crystals than Zimbabwe's annual gemstone export

At the bottom of your heart you know that no matter how hard you've tried, you couldn't get closer to enlightenment with any of these practices.

Awakening is like sunglasses


Imagine you’ve lost your favourite sunglasses one day. You’ve turned the house upside down to find them but you’ve had no luck. You have been searching for them for hours that feels longer than a holiday with your in-laws on a deserted island in the monsoon season. Yuck!

Finally, as you walked past your mirror you suddenly noticed that those damn shades were on the top of your head from the very beginning. Moral of the story?

a) Never go on holiday with your in-laws.
b) Enlightenment is already within you, just like the sunglasses have been on the top of your head from all along.

As you can see awakening is far easier than you can imagine. But if you choose to, it can be a wild ride. It can challenge your limitations, crush all your illusions and it's definitely not meant to be for those who want to keep their eyes closed. The road to enlightenment is not for you if you want to stay in your old world or if you still want to be involved in your daily dramas.








And it's definitely not for the spiritual sourpusses

Enlightenment is for those courageous souls, who dare to take at least 128% responsibility for all parts of their life
and not afraid of laugh their butt off when it's time to face their shadows.



I'm a sourpuss I'm fearless






The Masters' #1 secret

Now that we've got rid of the sourpusses, let's talk about the real shizz- how to be awakened? Actually my answer is rather boring:

You don't need to do anything to achieve enlightenment. Yeah, yeah chanting and meditating in the Himalayas sound awesome, but please... all you need to do is let Consciousness embrace you.

So get ready to throw your self-help books in the fire! You don't need positive affirmations anymore! You don't even need my artworks to awake you. Hanging my paintings on your wall won't grant you a free ticket to Nirvana... although it can direct you towards personal enlightenment.

Signs of Awakening

As you are going through awakening there could be days when you can feel a new level of connectedness with the universe. Your body feels lighter, your intuition heightened and everything is unicorns and rainbows.

And there could be other days when the shit hits the fan and you are likely to encounter a phenomenon which I call the awakening challenges.

These can come in the shape of inexplicable feelings of loneliness, sadness, body aches, physical ungroundedness or a total loss of joy and passion of life. These are just old karmic patterns from your past so the best thing you can do is let go all of those craps.




Get out of your mind

My art helps you releasing all the old emotional, karmic junks that won’t serve your life anymore. The sole purpose of my paintings is soothing your awakening challenges.

They are made for conscious humans to help them see the light during their dark night of the soul.

Every artwork on this website contains channeled messages for your Soul. So if any of my painting triggers something in you, it’s because you are ready to realize your enlightenment.

If you find these pictures totally unconnected to you it simply means that you haven’t experienced awakening in your life yet.

Long time no see


You probably forgot everything so let me tell you about our previous encounter:

I was preparing for my next incarnation into planet Earth with a group of awesome Souls who were my long time friends from many-many lifetimes before. During the great excitement of figuring out our next lives on this planet, doubts arose amongst us: What if Earth's energies will be too challenging for us to carry out our plans? What if we can’t realize our enlightenment in this lifetime?

So I had made a decision: when I come to Earth I will become an artist channeling the New Energies into paintings so that they will remind you of who you really are and why you’ve come here.

There is a reason why you've found this website. It’s not a coincidence that our paths have crossed. If any artwork here inspires you and you feel that reading these lines touches your heart, it’s the solid proof that we’ve met before:

You are one of those Souls with whom we decided that this time we are going to go all in for enlightenment! In this life, in this body, on this planet. You can't imagine, my friend, how thrilled I am to know that we are finally meeting again!


Still can’t get enough of me?

I skipped the small talk before, I get it. So here are some random facts about me:

  • I’ve travelled across Europe as a pet sitter. When I’m not drawing, you will most likely find me next to a pool in a hammock accompanied by my furry friends.
  • I’m a sucker for succulents, horticulturally insane & proud mother of many plants.
  • When I was young I wanted to be a herbal whitch and live in the forest. As I a grown up I still enjoy wild camping under the stars.
  • Hint, hint... You can bribe me anytime with a jar of Nutella … or with rare Japanese comics.
  • But most importantly, I'm here to help you ignite your spark of Consciousness, so come and let's dance together at the campfire!



So what are you waiting for?

Let my artwork be like your first coffee in the morning that wakes you up, opens your eyes and pulls you out of this sleep-walking zombie mode.
