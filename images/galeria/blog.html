How to get rid of your karma?

Do you feel that your karmic baggage you’ve been carrying forever is dragging you down? Did you get stuck in certain situations and old habits that won’t serve you anymore? Carry on reading because I’m going to reveal how to break through the cycle of your karma in an instant. Read more...

According to the theory you can accumulate karma from your past lives or you can build it in your present life. The Law of Karma states that every action must have a reaction.

"Through our actions we plant the “seeds” of our future destiny and therefore whatever happens to us is the fruit of our own past."

Generally if you think and act positively that generates good karma and bad deeds cause future suffering, which might seem to you as some kind of punishment, you need to endure the consequences until you pay off your bad karma.

There is another theory that explains bad karma as an equivalent to a spiritual lesson that you need to experience in order to understand your acts. They claim that you’d never understand the consequences of your actions, unless someone does the same thing to you.

So the question is:

Is karma a punishment or is it a spiritual lesson? And most importantly, how to shake off your karma in a second?

Let me give you an example: Sarah slaps Anna on the face. It hurts Anna a lot.

What has happened here by the terms of karma?


Is karma a punishment?

If we examine the situation from this point of view we can declare that Sarah has earned some bad karma by hurting Anna. If Anna slaps Sarah back, then their karmic connection would be balanced out again. End of story.

But if Anna won’t slap back, we can assume that Sarah might be “slapped back” sooner or later in her life. Or she might bring her karmic debt forward into her next life -for example- when she would choose a violent husband who would beat her up once in a while so that Sarah’s karmic debt will finally be settled.

And who knows, it could be Anna who had incarnated into the role of the husband? It doesn’t really matter, anyways.

So let’s move on from there!


Is karma a spiritual lesson?

Sarah has slapped Anna so that Anna has an opportunity to learn how it feels to be hurt moreover, Sarah can learn about how to express her anger. In the next cycle they might incarnated back in a reversed role.

Or could it be that it was already the second round when Sarah hit Anna and Anna was the original abuser?

Who knows? And it doesn’t really matter after all. So let’s move on and make some sort of conclusion.


Is karma an illusion?

Sarah has slapped Anna but what if it hurts Sarah as much as it hurts Anna?

Let’s say your left hand hits your right hand. What would you say to yourself?

-“Ouuch, you poor little right hand! Shame on you bastard left hand, you will definitely pay for your evil deed!”

Ehh, it totally wouldn’t make any sense, would it?

Now I have a question to ask you: who do you identify yourself? Are you just your right hand or are you your left hand? Or are you both?

I guess you would say that you are not just your hands but you are your whole body.

*Side-note* you are way more than just your body, but let’s put that aside for another discussion now…

Let’s get back to the topic. You are identifying yourself with your body as a whole. And both of your hands are part of your body, so when your left hand slapped your right hand you had become both the abuser and the victim at the same time.

You create all of your life experiences

As an enlightened master and a creator being, you create your own universe consciously or unconsciously as well as your own life and all of your experiences. And because the whole universe is your creation, Sarah and Anna both are your creation, too.

Yes, from a limited point of view they seem to be two separate beings but actually Sarah and Anna are both part of the very same source– they are part of you, just like both hands are part of the your body.

So at this point we can understand why karma’s existence is pointless. If someone does something to someone else, they will experience both the act and the counteract at the same time. So there would absolutely no need for karma at this point.

Yes, I said, there is no need for karma to exist. It’s just a big, fat illusion to start with

-“Ok, then Essie tell me why are so many of us, spiritual seekers are so burdened with karma? And no matter how much we chant, yoga and meditate, karma is still sticking to us like a nasty tick?”

So are you asking me...

How to get rid of an illusion?

First, I want you to realize that karma doesn't exist, it's just an illusion. It only feels real for you because you want it to be real.

You are only allowing karma in your life because you are actually enjoying it. Yes, you heard it right you still love to play with karma and suffer from karma. Otherwise karma wouldn’t be there.

Oh, I hear you cursing at me, telling me that all you want to do is to clear off your karmic debts...

If that’s really true then all you need to do is transcend your karma. To go beyond karma.

Don’t battle with it, don’t run from it and don’t be a victim of it. Just take some deep breaths and clearly choose that you don’t want karma to be part of your life anymore. That’s all folks!





P.S. I know there could be some super-challenging situations when shitstorms come and go and sweep your awareness away like autumn leaves.

During one of those shit-tornados, I know it’s a tad bit difficult to stay in the present moment and it’s also easy to think that you are just a poor victim of life.

I painted this picture back in 2015 and named her “Priestess of the Northern Star” . And even though it’s an older painting, it’s still one of my favourites.

During the last couple of years I’ve noticed something strange: whenever I looked at her another name popped into my mind: “Karmic Princess”. Back then, I had no idea what the connection was between her and the karma but whenever I took a glimpse at her I felt at ease.

At that time when I was painting it I was in the middle of cleansing a lot of my personal karma. People turned up from past lives and turned my life into chaos. So to calm down the turmoil I was intuitively creating “Karmic Princess” to soothe my energies.

I unconsciously invited in the energies of higher consciousness and painted this picture to help myself remember my true self and to help me return to Awareness. And also to be your reminder throughout your daily challenges that you are the Master, you are the Presence and you always have a choice to go beyond your karmic craps.
